41449 DAVID-TC DOWN

DAVID-TC DOWN: Parka in David-TC Down. Starting with an initial star-shaped Japanese polyester/polyamide substrate, garments in DAVID-TC are assembled and then simultaneously dyed and treated with an anti-drop agent. During garment dyeing under pressure at 130°C, the heat radically transforms the structure and feel of the material. The result is beautiful and wearable clothing that combine an industrial feel with a rich and unique tactile experience.
